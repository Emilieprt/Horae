Our values

We are a fair trade women's shoe brand. The materials used in the manufacturing process are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. We also use recycled and recyclable materials afterwards.

We care about the well-being of our employees and their happiness at work. We respect the standards and cultures of each of our employees located in several countries. They provide us with natural materials.

However the manufacture of the shoes is exclusively French. Our employees are qualified and constantly follow training courses in order to improve their skills.

Don't forget to visit our social network pages to find out more about us.

Enjoy your visit to our site!
